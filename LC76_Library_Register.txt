               commitments in plain field-voice (a journey of movement,
               attention, and becoming). Reads standalone; no apparatus.
               Companion to the Commentary. v4. Registered 14 June 2026.

  LC76_Overlanders_Manifesto_Commentary.html   ON GITHUB
               Sixteen companion essays naming the thinkers beneath each
               commitment (Bergson, Weil, Lugones, Versfeld, Jullien, et al.),
               a map of six held tensions, a Jullien coda, and a full reference
               list. Companion to the Manifesto. v4. Registered 14 June 2026.

  LC76_History_of_Overlanding.html             ON GITHUB
               The long way round — where the practice came from: 1830s
               drovers and Bertha Benz through Barbara Toy, Tom Sheppard, and
               the Camel Trophy to the modern boom. Closes with where we stand
               and where our ethos parts from the mainstream. Companion to the
               Manifesto. Registered 23 June 2026.

  LC76_Expedition_as_a_System.html             ON GITHUB
               Six essays on seeing the whole — Meadows, Ackoff, Capra,
               Bateson, Perrow, Korzybski & Seneca distilled for the driver's
               seat: synthesis before analysis; the web not the ladder;
               dancing with systems (feedback over prediction); stocks &
               delays (the slow cascade); leverage points (the veto rule,
               goals, "eco not ego" as paradigm); seams & the second question;
               coda — the map is not the territory. Commentary essay voice &
               design system; references keyed by essay. Philosophical root
               of R41 and R38 §6. Companion to the Manifesto & R41.
               Registered 12 July 2026 (OI-23 Option B, owner-directed).

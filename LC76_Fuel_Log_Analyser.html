Headline economy Units
📐
Reading these numbers. Median — the middle reading; half your tanks did better, half worse (outlier-proof, best for planning). Mean — the plain average (total ÷ count); one freak tank skews it. P90 / P10 — percentiles: 90% of tanks fall below P90 (your realistically-good end), only 10% fall below P10 (your realistically-bad end — use it for conservative remote legs). Band — the raw best-to-worst spread across every interval, outliers included.

Cost — segmented by price regime
ℹ
Why segmented: Angola diesel is heavily subsidised (~R7/L equivalent) and recorded in a different price regime to home (ZAR ~R20–33/L). Blending them into one R/L or R/km figure would be misleading, so they are kept separate. The km/L economy figures above are currency-independent and can be read across both.

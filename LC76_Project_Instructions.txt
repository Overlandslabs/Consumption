This project builds and maintains the LC76 Overland Reference Library for Neil
& Mary-Ann's 2009 Toyota Land Cruiser 76 Series SW (HZJ76R-RKMRS), based in
South Africa. The vehicle is used for serious overlanding across Southern and
East Africa, with a planned two-year, 60,000+ km expedition in 2028–2030 (distance figure
confirmed authoritative 9 Jul 2026 — supersedes the ~40,000 km working figure).
En-route service policy (owner, 9 Jul 2026): follow the service schedule at
Toyota dealers where the route allows; oil changes are never deferred — every
en-route oil change is also an RA-2 oil-analysis sampling point.

Library hosted at: overlandslabs.github.io/Consumption

- Masthead standard (DG v1.6, section S10) — six slots, fixed order:
  back-link (⌂ Index) · eyebrow (Collection · ID) · title (Title Case) ·
  one-line subtitle (≤~12 words) · invariant identity line · Last-updated date.
  Identity line, identical on every file: "2009 LC76 SW · 1HZ 4.2L Diesel ·
  low-boost turbo (0.6 bar) · intercooler". NEVER in a masthead: odometer, GVM,
  per-document component specs, the EX-programme tag, or a vehicle code in the
  eyebrow (those live in Facts §2 / R1 / the body). Closed collection vocabulary
  (six): Reference · Country Profile · Terrain Profile · Field Tool · Philosophy ·
  Itinerary. Profiles carry ONE extra element — native official name as a
  de-emphasised line under the title; their entry/visa/plating strip moves to the
  body (§Entry/Border). Exempt: calculators (no back-link/identity/date, eyebrow
  "Field Tool") and governance/infrastructure (index.html, Design Guidelines,
  Profile Template, audit HTML). Date form: "Last updated: D Month YYYY"
  (lowercase, full month, no zero-pad) — replaces "Last Updated"/"Compiled".
- Sticky nav: masthead and TOC MUST be wrapped together in a single sticky
  container (position:sticky; top:0; z-index:100). Never separate.
- Content width: max-width:100% with percentage padding
  (padding: 28px 5% 80px). NEVER a fixed pixel max-width cap. Headers,
  footers, sticky nav always full browser width.
- TOC navigation: JS scrollIntoView / computed-offset scrollTo (not href
  anchors). Scroll offset must account for full sticky container height.
  IntersectionObserver for active-state highlighting.
- Footer convention (post-standardisation, 8 Jul 2026):
  · Reference docs, profiles (body), philosophy, itinerary: NO footer.
  · Profiles: single-line sources-attribution footer only
    (`<div class="doc-footer"><div>Sources: ...</div></div>`).
  · Calculators: functional footer RETAINED (live JS readout values).
  · Audit reports: provenance footer RETAINED (governance).
  · index.html: minimal brand line RETAINED.
  · Do NOT add footers to new documents unless they carry genuinely unique
    functional content (live values, source attributions, governance audit trail).

  {overflow-x:hidden; overflow-x:clip; overscroll-behavior-x:none} — on html,

Two-vehicle rule CKGR interior and Mabuasehube: two vehicles strongly recommended. A single immobilised vehicle is 200+ km from any assistance. This note must appear in any profile section covering these zones.
FMD document — mandatory for northern circuit A Foot-and-Mouth Disease cross-cordon movement permit is required to move from southern to northern Botswana (across the Buffalo Fence). Obtain at the vet cordon gate. No raw meat into the northern zone.

Currency at Botswana park gates BWP, ZAR, USD, GBP accepted at DWNP gates — notes only, no coins, no cards (card machines present but unreliable). Camp fees paid separately to private operators (BigFoot, SKL, etc.) — usually paid before arrival. Both park entry voucher AND camp booking confirmation must be presented at gate. Campsite fees are NOT paid at the gate.

Pan crust warning — applies to all pan references in Botswana profile Pan surface looks firm but hides soft mud below. Test on foot before driving any new section. Variable crust strength — check satellite imagery (Google Earth historical) and iOverlander recent reports. After rain: stay off pans for minimum 3 days. Vehicle recovery from pan interior is a major multi-vehicle operation.

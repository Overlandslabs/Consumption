Fuel qualitysee also §07 Fuel

Lead with the durable fact: the 1HZ is indifferent to sulphur grade — indirect injection, mechanically governed, no DPF, no common-rail tolerances. The real risks are water and particulate contamination and low pump turnover. State the country-specific concern, then the discipline: pre-filter and water separator, drain schedule, spare filters, log every fill for the R3 ledger and the R23 §7 review. Doctrine owned by R24.
